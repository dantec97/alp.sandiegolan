What is Automated LAN Party?

ALP bundles all the games and files needed for clients in one package. Build your VM, use our .VHDX drive, and hit start.
The built-in tournament, calendar, and download pages take a load off LAN party admins. Attendees browse the intranet page for all their information.

What ALP is not!

ALP is not intended for online service or server use. While you can port-forward for game servers, multiple places running the same software may cause conflicts in game browsers.
ALP is not a web-forum or registration site prior to LANs and does not have a seating chart.
